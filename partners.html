Here are some of our partners

Integrated Shipping Services

Integrated Shipping Nigeria Limited (Intership) is a leading Nigerian shipping agency.  Intership complements our expertise in vessel ownership and operations by offering a comprehensive range of services including port agency, logistics, and consulting. This strategic partnership allows our clients to benefit from a one-stop shop for all their maritime logistics needs.

Oceanic Marine Management Limited

Sea Transport Services Limited (STS) bolsters its crew management capabilities through its partnership with Oceanic Marine Management Limited.  Oceanic Marine is a specialist in vessel manning and technical management, a critical aspect of safe and efficient ship operations.

Our expertise in vessel ownership, chartering, and brokerage is complemented by Oceanic Marine's ability to provide highly qualified crew members and ensure vessels are maintained to the highest technical standards.  Additionally, Oceanic Marine's focus on safety and compliance aligns with our values, guaranteeing clients peace of mind knowing their vessels are operated by a professional and reliable crew.
