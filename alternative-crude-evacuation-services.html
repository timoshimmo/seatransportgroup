Alternative
Crude Evacuation Services

Explore a new world of efficiency and cost-effectiveness with our Alternative Crude Evacuation Services. Our experts tailor evacuation plans to optimise efficiency, reduce costs, and minimise environmental impact.

We go beyond traditional methods

STS specializes in Alternative Crude Evacuation (ACE) services, offering a robust and reliable alternative to pipeline transportation. Our customized solutions leverage logistics expertise and a modern fleet of marine vessels to ensure the safe, efficient, and cost-effective evacuation of your wet or dry crude oil to your preferred location.

We offer turnkey evacuation solutions, including barging, mooring services, and vessel security, designed to eliminate potential losses and ensure smooth operations.  With our proven expertise and commitment to safety, STS is your trusted partner for all your crude oil evacuation needs.
