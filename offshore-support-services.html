Safe and Efficient Anchor Handling and Recovery

Our AHTS vessels are more than just workhorses; they're specially outfitted to handle various anchor handling needs.  For instance, their capabilities are particularly valuable for cable laying vessels which often require specialised assistance due to the large size and weight of their anchors.

Our highly skilled crew and advanced anchor handling vessels ensure precise positioning, mooring, and station keeping for your critical offshore operations, minimising downtime, maximising safety, and navigating challenges with confidence.
